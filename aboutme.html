About Me ‌ Throughout my childhood, I was fascinated by how computer programs and video games functioned. I spent my middle school days studying in my school's computer lab making rudimentary games in Scratch before going on to learn more formal programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and Python. Later, I became more interested in seeing how robots worked in tandem with programming which led me to joining my high school's robotics team and becoming the Construction Leader of the team. However, it was also during high school when I began working with machine learning tools and discovered my passion for working with machine learning. My academic journey has led me to Stony Brook where I continue to study Computer Science.

In my free time, I enjoy reading science fiction books and playing video games. My current favorite sci-fi series is Dune which my friends have heard me ramble on about for hours. I also enjoy being active and can often be found in the gym working out or playing badminton with my friends.
